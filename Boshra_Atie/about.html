About Sylvester

Why I Named Him Sylvester

My mom is a huge fan of Disney and classic cartoons, and the moment we saw him, the name was obvious. He looks almost exactly like Sylvester from the Looney Tunes series "Sylvester and Tweety" and just like the cartoon character, he even has a small white dot at the very tip of his tail. That little detail is what sealed the name for us.

His Look A Tuxedo with Cow Print

Most tuxedo cats have a simple black and white pattern, but Sylvester's markings are scattered like a cow's spots, giving him a one of a kind appearance that always gets comments from people who meet him.

Quick Facts

  • Age: 6 years old
  • Breed: Domestic Shorthair
  • Pattern: Tuxedo with cow print
  • Favorite spot: The sunny windowsill

His One and Only Trick

Sylvester only knows one trick shaking hands but he performs it with full confidence every single time.
